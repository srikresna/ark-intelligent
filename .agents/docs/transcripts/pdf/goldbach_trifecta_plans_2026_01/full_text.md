GOLDBACH TRIFECTA
A WIREFRAME FOR TRADING CME DRIVEN PRICE CYCLES
TRADE PLANS BY HOPIPLAKA

LIQUIDITY:
The liquidity plan is all about engineering and taking liquidity.
You will see price hover above a range low, or below a range high, and building liquidity.
You want to monitor levels [11-89] and [3-97], as well as the range extreme [0-100] and the LLOD [7-93].
Price will consolidate around these levels, maybe a little retrace to let people believe price will reverse.
This is just a trick to build liquidity. Price will be later repriced to scoop up this liquidity towards the external liquidity levels.

FLOW CONTINUATION:
Price will move through the flow layer. Here you will hone your patience skills.
When price touches the flow layer, shorts are preferred. You don't buy at this level, but anticipate a rejection.
They will first retrace price, back into the flow layer. You look for potential gap inside the flow layer.

FLOW REJECTION:
Price is coming from the rebalance layer, and moves into the flow layer.
The safer option is to wait for price to reject the flow layer, preferably the middle of it [29-71].
Price should aggressively reject the level, creating a gap to move outside the flow layer.

REBALANCE:
Price will consolidate around the middle of the rebalance layer, around the internal rebalance liquidity [47-53].
Stops or liquidity will be engineered on both sides of the middle of the rebalance layer.
When price hits the external liquidity of the rebalance layer [41-59], you enter the trade.

EINSTEIN:
Price consolidates around the high/low [0-100] of the range.
It will create a block in between levels [3-97] and [11-89].
Price will aggressively move higher, creating a gap in between levels [11-89] and [17-83].
You enter the trade at level [17-83], or at the gap created in between the [11-89] and [17-83] zone.
You take partials at the internal rebalance liquidity [47-53], or close the trade completely.

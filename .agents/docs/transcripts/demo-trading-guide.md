## Full Content

This guide covers the fundamentals of professional trading, including risk management, entry strategies, and psychological discipline.

### Key Concepts

#### 1. Risk Management
- Never risk more than 2% per trade
- Always use stop losses
- Maintain risk-reward ratio of at least 1:2
- Position sizing is critical

#### 2. Entry Strategy
- Wait for confirmation signals
- Identify support and resistance levels
- Use multiple timeframes for analysis
- Enter on pullbacks to value areas

#### 3. Exit Strategy
- Take partial profits at key levels
- Trail stop losses to protect gains
- Exit when thesis is invalidated
- Never move stop loss further away

#### 4. Psychology
- Control emotions during losses
- Stick to your trading plan
- Review trades daily
- Maintain discipline consistently

### Trading Rules

1. Only trade during high liquidity hours
2. Wait for price to reach key levels
3. Confirm with volume analysis
4. Never chase the price
5. Always have a predefined exit plan

### Common Mistakes

- Overtrading
- Revenge trading after losses
- Not using stop losses
- Ignoring risk-reward ratios
- Emotional decision making

### Conclusion

Successful trading requires discipline, proper risk management, and consistent execution of your strategy. Focus on the process, not just the outcome.
